About

Maria Ershova is a product leader who specializes in early stage companies. She's currently the Product Manager at SAGE Campus and previously Maria was a startup co-founder.

Maria joined SAGE Campus as a first product manager and successfully pivoted the platform from B2C to B2B within first two months.

Prior to this, Maria learned product from Stanford University and co-founded a clean tech start-up in the USA.

Maria went to school at ESCP Business School and Warwick Business School. She grew up in Moscow and currenly lives in London.
